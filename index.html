  • If the dealer shows an Ace, you can elect to buy insurance against his possible blackjack for half your stake (pays 2-to-1).
  • Blackjack pays 3-to-2.
  • The objective is to beat the dealer's score without going over 21.
  • The dealer must hit until he reaches 17. He must also hit on 17 if it includes an ace (Soft 17).
  • Splitting breaks your hand into two hands, each of which you play before the dealer plays. (Normally only available when you have a pair, but I left it available on all hands for testing/demonstration purposes).
  • Surrendering forfeits the hand and results in a loss of half your stake.
  • Doubling down draws one more card and doubles your stake.
  • Hitting will add another card to your hand as long as you are under 21. Go over 21 and you lose!
  • Standing will lock in your score and turn the action to the dealer.
